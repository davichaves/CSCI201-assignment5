Instructions:
You begin the game in edit mode,click a square to place your ships.
You must also select a battle file.
Once you do this, you may begin the game!
Gameplay:
Click an ememy grid square to attack.
Gameplay will continue until one or both of the players have their ships destroyed.
Once this happens, one player will be declared the winner.

There are 4 types of ship.
A)Aircraft carrier x1 - 5squares long
B)Battleship x1 - 4 squares long
C)Cruiser x1 - 3 squares long
D)Destroyer x2 - 2 squares long

Thanks for playing!
